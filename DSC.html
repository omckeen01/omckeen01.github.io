Dean's Student Cabinet

Cabinet Overview

The Dean's Student Cabinet works with Dean Laura Kornish and provides opportunities for students to make tangible change in the Leeds School of Business through iniatives surrounding Leeds values.

There are 5 committees under Dean's Student Cabinet, one for each of Leeds values (Academic Excellence, Community, Inclusion, Integrity, Forward-Looking). These committees are made up of undergraduate Leeds students who are responsible for enacting iniatives they come up with.
